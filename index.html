About PyNEX

Successful Builds information Homepage
PyNEX - optimiz and powerful server software for Minecraft Bedrock Edition. PyNEX is much more powerful and more optimized than PocketMine-MP. The structure of the PyNEX kernel is similar to PocketMine, however the speed and optimization quality is better than PocketMine and Nukkit. PyNEX has a big advantage: it has multi-version, 1.7 and higher. You can download the latest build on GitHub or on the successful build page.To view information about authors and about development go to the information page.
